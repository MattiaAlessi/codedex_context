Find Data: Search Kaggle for "Steam Games" or "Global Cyber Security Index."

Analyze with Julius AI: Ask it, "Find the correlation between game popularity and vulnerability reports." This will give you the "Aha!" moment (e.g., "Cheaper games are 3x more likely to be targeted by hackers").

Visualize with Tableau: Take that insight and create a high-fidelity "Vulnerability Heatmap." Tableau Public is free and looks incredibly professional in a portfolio.

Storytell with ChatGPT: Paste your findings and ask it to draft a "Technical Brief" for a security firm. This gives you the "Best Presentation" edge.
